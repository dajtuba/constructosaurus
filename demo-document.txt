SAMPLE CONSTRUCTION DOCUMENT - RESIDENTIAL FOUNDATION

PROJECT: Rogers House Addition
DRAWING: S-101 Foundation Plan
DATE: 2026-02-08

GENERAL NOTES:
1. Use 3/4" plywood throughout for formwork
2. All concrete shall be 3000 PSI minimum
3. Provide 24 expansion joints at locations shown
4. Install (12) #4 rebar @ 12" o.c. in foundation walls

FOUNDATION SPECIFICATIONS:

Phase 1 - Excavation and Footings:
- Excavate to 4'-0" depth
- Compact subgrade with 6" crushed stone base
- Install 2x8 PT lumber forms for footings
- Place 15 cubic yards concrete for footings
- Embed (8) anchor bolts @ 24" o.c.

Phase 2 - Foundation Walls:
- Construct 8" CMU foundation walls
- Use Type S mortar throughout
- Install 200 linear feet of 8" CMU blocks
- Provide #4 rebar vertical @ 48" o.c.
- Provide #4 rebar horizontal @ 16" o.c.
- Apply waterproofing membrane to exterior

Phase 3 - Slab on Grade:
- Place 4" gravel base
- Install 6 mil poly vapor barrier
- Place 10x10 W1.4xW1.4 welded wire fabric
- Pour 4" concrete slab, 3000 PSI
- Estimated 12 cubic yards concrete for slab

MATERIAL CALLOUTS:
(1) Foundation drain: 4" perforated PVC pipe, 100 linear feet
(2) Sill plate: 2x6 PT lumber, 80 linear feet
(3) Anchor bolts: 1/2" x 10", galvanized, 16 each
(4) Rebar: #4 grade 60, 500 linear feet total

TEMPORARY MATERIALS:
- Formwork: 3/4" plywood, 40 sheets
- Form ties: 200 each
- Scaffolding: As required by contractor

FINISHES:
- Exterior waterproofing: 200 SF
- Interior dampproofing: 200 SF

CONFLICTS WITH ARCHITECTURAL DRAWINGS:
- A-101 shows slab thickness as 5" (use 4" per structural)
- A-102 shows different anchor bolt spacing (use 24" o.c. per structural)

ADDENDUM #1 (2026-02-07):
